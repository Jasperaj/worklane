Top 4 Essential Apps for Finance Professionals in Nepal

In Nepal's rapidly evolving financial landscape, staying informed and efficient is crucial for professionals. Leveraging the right digital tools can significantly enhance productivity and ensure compliance. Here are four indispensable apps every finance professional in Nepal should consider:

1. IRD Nepal App

Stay updated with the latest tax notices, including critical updates like "No date extension for Income Tax Filing" and new billing formats. The official Inland Revenue Department app provides access to:

  • New notices
  • PAN/VAT details
  • Tax clearance statuses
  • TDS verifications

Ensure compliance and streamline your tax management processes.

Download for Android Download for iOS

2. Kaanun - Laws of Nepal App

Navigating Nepal's corporate legal landscape becomes effortless with Kaanun. Whether you're recalling sections about promoter requirements for Public Limited Companies or other legal provisions, this app offers:

  • Offline access to major legislation
  • Comprehensive coverage of the Companies Act, Income Tax Act, Labour Act, and more

Make informed decisions with ease.

Download for Android

3. Google Sheets / Microsoft Excel

For on-the-go financial analysis and reporting:

  • Open, view, and edit Excel files anytime, anywhere
  • Utilize advanced functions and real-time collaboration
  • Automate tasks to enhance productivity and accuracy
